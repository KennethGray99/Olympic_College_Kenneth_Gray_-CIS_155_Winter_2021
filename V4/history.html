The History of the Shiba Inu

The Shiba Inu is a native Japanese dog breed that dates all the way back around 300 B.C. The fox like looking dog is small and has a thick double coat and curved tail. Shiba Inus were known for being a hunting dog that chased wild prey. Knowing what Shiba Inu means further explains the nature of the dog, "Shiba" meaning brushwood and "Inu" meaning dog.

Dating to around 2013, the Shiba Inu has become a well-known meme "Doge". The image on the left was the popular meme image of the Doge or the Shiba named Kabosu. Shiba Inus have continued growing in popularity over the years coming from Japan to the United States.
